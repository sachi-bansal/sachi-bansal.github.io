Biosynthetic Knee Endoprosthesis

Overview

Restoring Knee Extension

This project addresses a fundamental challenge in endoprosthetic knee reconstruction: reliably restoring the extensor mechanism after surgery. I am leading the design, validation, and development of a novel biosynthetic knee endoprosthesis that improves soft tissue-to-metal integration through a biological intermediary, aimed at improving both functional outcomes and long-term device longevity.

This research is currently in progress. Check back soon for updates.
