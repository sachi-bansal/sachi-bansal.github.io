Biomarker Research

Digital Biomarker Algorithm Development

I developed a novel algorithm and performed data analysis on a cohort of 15 participants to uncover correlations between digital biomarkers, supporting early-stage evaluation of non-invasive wellness detection capabilities in consumer wearable devices.

Validation Studies

Internal Study Design & Execution

I designed and executed two internal validation studies: one to acquire reference physiological data and train an algorithm for continuous vital sign monitoring, and one to establish ground truth sensor measurements to evaluate sensor accuracy and characterize relationships between device signals and physiological shifts. I developed full study protocols, managed end-to-end participant data collection, and delivered performance findings to engineering and management partners.
